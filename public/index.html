E-Waste Sokoban

Instructions

Welcome to Sokoban Solver! Little Timmy needs to push the Recycling Blocks to the Broken Laptops to properly dispose of E-Waste!.

1. Use the directional buttons to move the Little Timmy.

2. You may reset the level as many times as you wish.

3. Once you have completed all three levels, show your end screen to the booth staff.

Complete 3 Levels to win a Tote Bag!
